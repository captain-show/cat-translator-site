FAQ

What does Cat Translator actually do?

Cat Translator analyzes feline vocalizations and translates them into human-readable messages, helping cat owners better understand their pet's needs, moods, and personality quirks.

Can this app really understand my cat?

While it doesn't decode feline language with 100% accuracy (they're mysterious creatures, after all!), it uses behavioral and acoustic data to offer the most likely interpretations based on tone, pitch, and context.

Does it work with kittens or older cats?

Yes! The app works across age groups. Kittens may have higher-pitched meows, while older cats may be quieter or more expressive in body language — Cat Translator adapts to both.

How do I record my cat's meow?

Just tap the "record" button while your cat vocalizes, and the app will capture the sound, analyze its pattern, and deliver a translation within seconds.

Can it translate purring or growling?

Yes! Purring may indicate comfort or excitement, while growling usually signals annoyance or fear. The app offers interpretations for a variety of sound types beyond basic meows.

Does it require internet access?

Most core features work offline, but advanced analysis and updates (like new vocal samples) may require a connection.

Is it safe for my cat?

Absolutely! Cat Translator only listens passively — it doesn't emit any sounds or signals. Just make sure your feline friend isn't trying to chew your phone out of curiosity!
